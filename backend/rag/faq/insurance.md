# Insurance Information - ABC Hospital

ABC Hospital accepts the following insurance providers:

- Jubilee Health Insurance
- Adamjee HealthCare
- Alfalah Takaful
- State Life Health
- IGI Life
- TPL Insurance
- EFU Health

Insurance Guidelines:
- Bring your original CNIC and insurance card.
- Pre-authorization may be required for some procedures.
- Emergency cases are processed immediately.

If unsure, ask the assistant: “Does my insurance cover X?”
