# Question Flow: Headache

Ask the following:

1. When did the headache start?
2. Where exactly is the pain? (front, back, sides)
3. What type of pain? (throbbing, pressure, sharp)
4. Any nausea or vomiting?
5. Any sensitivity to light or sound?
6. Are you having fever?
7. Did you miss sleep or have stress?
8. Have you taken any medicine?

Red Flags:
- Worst headache of life
- Sudden severe onset
- Vision loss or confusion
