# Question Flow: Fever

Ask these questions one by one:

1. When did the fever start?
2. How high was the temperature (if measured)?
3. Is the fever constant or comes and goes?
4. Do you have chills or shivering?
5. Do you have headache, nausea, or fatigue?
6. Any sore throat, cough, or body pain?
7. Any recent travel or contact with sick people?
8. Did you take any medicine for fever?
