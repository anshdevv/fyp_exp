# Question Flow: Cough

Ask in this order:

1. How long have you had the cough?
2. Is the cough dry or with phlegm?
3. If phlegm present: What color is it? (yellow, green, blood?)
4. Do you have fever?
5. Any shortness of breath?
6. Any chest pain while coughing?
7. Do you smoke?
8. Is the cough worse at night or morning?
9. Any contact with TB patients?

Red Flags:
- Coughing blood
- Severe breathing difficulty

Rules:
- One question at a time.
- No diagnosis.
